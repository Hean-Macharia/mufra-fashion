Terms and Conditions
1. Order Acceptance

Your order is subject to acceptance by MUFRA FASHIONS. We reserve the right to refuse service to anyone for any reason at any time.

2. Pricing and Payment

All prices are in Kenyan Shillings (KES). Payment must be made in full before order processing. Delivery fees vary by location: KES 100 within Embu, KES 200 elsewhere. Free shipping for orders over KES 5,000 within Embu.

3. Shipping and Delivery

Orders are typically processed within 24 hours and delivered within 3-5 business days. Delivery times may vary based on location and availability.

4. Returns & Refunds

Returns accepted within 30 days of delivery. Items must be in original condition with tags attached. Refunds are processed within 7-14 business days.

5. Privacy Policy

We respect your privacy. Your personal information is used only for order processing and delivery. We never share your information with third parties.
